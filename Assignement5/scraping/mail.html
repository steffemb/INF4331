Our Technology

Lucidtech is developing state-of-the-art machine learning technology for document data extraction and validation in the financial industry.

Our technology is fast, reliable and highly scalable, and can easily be integrated in existing software solutions through our API. Our core technology is constantly improving, and with an ever increasing dataset we continue to push the boundaries to provide the best solutions for our customers.
